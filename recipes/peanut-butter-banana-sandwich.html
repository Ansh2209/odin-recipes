Peanut Butter and Banana Sandwich

Description

A quick and tasty snack, the Peanut Butter & Banana Sandwich combines creamy peanut butter with sweet, ripe banana slices. Itâ€™s perfect for breakfast, a mid-day snack, or anytime you need a little energy boost. Simple, healthy, and ready in just a few minutes.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 banana

Steps

  1. Spread peanut butter on one slice of bread.
  2. Slice banana and place on top.
  3. Cover with the other slice. Serve!
