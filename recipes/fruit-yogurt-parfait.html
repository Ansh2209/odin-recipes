Fruit Yogurt Parfait

Description

This refreshing Fruit Yogurt Parfait layers creamy yogurt, crunchy granola, and colorful fresh fruits. It’s a light, nutritious treat that’s as beautiful as it is delicious. Great for breakfast or a healthy dessert.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1/2 cup granola
  • 1/2 cup mixed fruits (strawberries, banana, blueberries)

Steps

  1. Layer yogurt, granola, and fruits in a glass.
  2. Repeat layers if desired.
  3. Serve chilled.
